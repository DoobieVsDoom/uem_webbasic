Umnotho Ebantwini Movement

  • Meet the Founder and President of UEM

  • Umnotho Ebantwini Greetings Colleagues. It is indeed a great pleasure to launch UEM Times, which will share the relevant details of how we are progressing as a Movement. In today's extract, we meet Dr Duduzile Mohlomi, Founder and President.

  • Dr Duduzile Mohlomi is the founder and brainchild of Umnotho Ebantwini Movement. She is a passionate servant leader that always puts her people first. Dr Mohlomi was the 6th child in her family and grew up with her siblings in the township of Kwa Mashu where she went on to complete her primary and high-school education. She comes from a working-class family having been raised by a mother who was a dress maker and widow.

  • Dr Mohlomi is a greatly respected and dynamic leader. Her friends, family and colleagues describe her as a loving and generous woman who wants to see everyone prospering in every manner. Furtermore, she is a woman who raised strong children yet all humble and God fearing.

  • Unotho Ebantwini is a GODLY VISION. which Dr Mohlomi obeyed as one chosen to be the instrument of God that brings joy, peace and happiness to all our peoples. That is why she strongly believes that the UEM Vision will never die no matter what people can try to do to thwart it. Isaiah 61:1 keeps Dr Mohlomi going.
