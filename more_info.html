Vision

Create new suppliers of goods and services amongst marginalised communities; trading more vibrantly within.

Mission

Stimulate and facilitate the transition from a consumption to a production led local economy that consumes what it produces, and is a growing contributor in the global supply chain.

Primary Objectives

Recognising 'low hanging fruits' as opportunities that are achievble in a Short-Term, we look to achieve the following...

Mobilise the South African black majority to buy from their own. Whether you are in the suburbs, townships, rural areas or CBD's, try to buy from black businesses. We have presence in all provinces and since black South Africans make up the largest demographic group, we will not be apologetic about it.

Develop presence in all provinces within Suburbs, Townships, Rural Areas and CBD's. The Township Economy has become a household phrase in South Africa but we need more than that. We will equip our people with skills so that factories can be re-established and land can be cultivated.

Successfully induct 1 Million black South Africans, being the largest demographic, into the business network for black South Africans. Let us create black owned South African brands and products and distribute them to black owned establishments, as well as support black service providers.

FAQs

We all have questions and some of them might have been asked before, however, feel free to contact us if you still have questions.

What is required for one to join Umnotho Ebantwini? Sign Up by entering the required details. Depending on your preferred membership option, you will be required to pay the relevant amount and you will receive your membership confirmation in your email.

When and Where are the general meetings held? Members are advised to join their regional whatsapp groups after Signing Up, such that, a UEM sector/region leader can advise you as to where the General Meeting will be held for that particular period.

What are the benefits of UEM Membership? We provide skills through in-house programmes that any member can join. We also provide a secure network of existing businesses that are willing to work with members to help provide workplace opportunities.
